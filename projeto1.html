Achilles.BR

1-D Organic Facies Prediction Software

Achilles.BR is a Python-based software developed to Petrobras. This softwares is capable of evaluating potential marine source rock intervals by modelling organic carbon content (TOC), organic matter quality and assessing source rock maturity. It was developed by the "Projeto Ressurgência 3" held at the Laboratório de Oceanografia e Paleoceanografia at the Fluminense Federal University (UFF). In a next phase, Achilles.BR will be coupled as a plug-in to the StratBR software, a C++-based commercial software owned by Petrobras
